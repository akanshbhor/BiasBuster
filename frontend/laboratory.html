Testing for AI Bias

Welcome to BiasBuster! Try prompting the AI to see how the engine detects context, severity, and implicit stereotypes. Our multi-layer architecture includes:

  • The Educational Firewall: Prevents tooltips from showing when discussing bias academically.
  • The Contextual Tech Allowlist (RAG): Understands words like "master" or "blind" in IT contexts.
  • The PySpellChecker Obfuscation Engine: Catches typos and bypasses intentionally misspelled slurs.
  • The DeBERTa Cross-Encoder: Detects implicit roles and coded agentic/communal language.
